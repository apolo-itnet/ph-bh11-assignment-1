Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Our vision is to build a new Bangladesh with the following pillars:

innovation

Bangladesh is fostering innovation through a thriving startup ecosystem, ICT advancements, and government-backed initiatives.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is promoting sustainability through green initiatives, renewable energy, and climate-resilient infrastructure.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure is rapidly improving with major investments in transportation, energy, and urban development.

Empowerment

Bangladesh is driving empowerment through initiatives in education, gender equality, and skill development.
